Directions

Plan of the venue - click image to enlarge

General Location

The wedding will take place in Brodnica (see map below). If you are arriving from abroad, it is easiest to get there after landing in Warsaw or Gdańsk (around 3 hours by car from any of those). If you want to take a bus or train instead, there are good connections from Warsaw, Poznań and Gdańsk (Contact us if you need more information).

Wedding Ceremony

The wedding ceremony will be held at the Immaculate Conception Catholic Church in Brodnica at 15.30 on 22.06.2019.

Wedding Party

The wedding party will commence around 17.00 (22.06.2019) in the Rytebłota Resort & Spa near Brodnica. Transport from the church to the venue will be provided for those in need. When it comes to transport back after the party and/or place to stay overnight, please refer to the confirmation tab.

Accomodation

The accomodation will be provided at the same complex the wedding venue is located. It will be either in a double hotel room or 6-person cottages. If you are in need of accomodation, please make sure you indicate that in the confirmation form. More information can be found at the complex's page - Rytebłota.
